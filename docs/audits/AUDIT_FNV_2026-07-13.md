## Dimension Findings (by severity)

### MEDIUM

#### FNV-D7-01 — Ragdoll: non-simulated descendant bones render at the stale animated pose (detached extremities) · NEW
- **Location**: `byroredux/src/ragdoll.rs:324-377` (writeback), `byroredux/src/boot.rs:584-617/635-703/782` (anim → propagation → Late-writeback stage order)
- **Mechanism**: `ragdoll_writeback_system` (Stage::Late) overwrites `GlobalTransform` only on the 18 ragdoll-body bones, *after* PostUpdate propagation already recomputed every bone's global from its still-animated local `Transform`. `animation_system` is **not** gated on `RagdollActive` (no such reference in `systems/animation.rs`), so clips keep sampling. Any bone that is a descendant of a ragdoll body but not itself a body — on the vanilla FNV skeleton, the finger bones (children of `Bip01 [LR] Hand`) and toe bones — is computed as `animated_parent_global × child_local` and never re-derived from the simulated parent. Result: fingers/toes float at the pre-ragdoll pose, detached from the crumpling hands/feet. The scene graph is left internally inconsistent (`global ≠ parent_global × local`) for ragdoll bones.
- **Impact**: Visible on every ragdoll activation (`ragdoll <id>` / `m41-ragdoll.sh`). Debug-triggered today (not yet wired to gameplay death) so blast radius is the reference demo — but it is the exact output the PHYSAL reference slice exists to showcase.
- **Suggested fix**: Gate `animation_system` off for `RagdollActive` subtrees, then after writeback either re-run localized propagation over the ragdoll subtree, or write each ragdoll bone's *local* `Transform` (relative to its simulated parent) so standard propagation carries the pose to the fingers/toes next frame.

#### FNV-D3-01 — Per-light ambient fill lacks a point/spot gate (exterior sun adds normal-independent fill) · ALREADY TRACKED (#1914)
- **Location**: `crates/renderer/shaders/triangle.frag` (per-light loop ambient-fill term)
- **Status**: Existing OPEN issue **#1914** (REN-D2-01 / RL-03, `renderer`/`medium`/`bug`). Premise re-verified against current shader; **recorded here, not re-filed** per dedup protocol.
- **Mechanism**: the per-light ambient fill is missing its stated `lightType < 1.5` gate, so the exterior directional (sun) `GpuLight` injects an unshadowed, normal-independent fill on every fragment. FNV-relevant — every Mojave exterior carries the CLMT/WTHR sun as a directional light. Distinct from the correct light-count-independent `AMBIENT_FILL` floor at `triangle.frag:1750-1752` (REND-#1452).
- **Impact**: mild flat lift on exterior fragments facing away from the sun; cosmetic. Blast radius = all exterior cells.

### LOW

#### ANIM-D6-01 — `CycleType::Reverse` single-reflection ping-pong doesn't clamp a delta larger than the clip · NEW
- **Location**: `crates/core/src/animation/player.rs:76-90`, `crates/core/src/animation/stack.rs:170-184`
- **Mechanism**: Reverse-cycle overshoot folds once (`local_time = 2*duration - local_time` / `-local_time`) with no subsequent range check. If `delta = dt·speed·frequency > 2·duration` (frame hitch on a very short reverse clip, or large speed/frequency), one reflection isn't enough and `local_time` lands outside `[0,duration]`. The Loop arm at the same sites is hitch-safe via modulo; Reverse is not.
- **Impact**: transient one-frame out-of-range `local_time`; self-corrects because samplers clamp to endpoint keys → at most a one-frame pose snap. Reverse clips are uncommon in FNV (idles Loop, furniture Clamp), so blast radius is small.
- **Suggested fix**: triangle-wave fold over period `2·duration` (`rem_euclid` + half-period reflect) so any delta magnitude stays in range.

#### FNV-D7-02 — Skinned-mesh `WorldBound` doesn't track a ragdoll that leaves its origin · NEW
- **Location**: `byroredux/src/ragdoll.rs:324-377`, `byroredux/src/systems/bounds.rs`
- **Mechanism**: writeback moves bone globals but never the mesh root entity; `make_world_bound_propagation_system` derives the bound from `LocalBound × GlobalTransform` on the (untouched) mesh root, and ragdoll bones carry no `LocalBound`. A ragdoll that slides/falls away from spawn keeps the bind-pose bound and can be frustum-culled / get a stale TLAS bound while on-screen. Does **not** violate the Late-write/`LocalBound` invariant (ragdoll bones have no `LocalBound`).
- **Impact**: occasional cull / RT-bound pop for a ragdoll traveling far from spawn; minor for slice-1 in-place crumple.
- **Suggested fix**: when a `RagdollActive` actor's bodies leave the bind-pose radius, expand/recenter the mesh `WorldBound` from live bone globals (or mark dirty).

#### FNV-D7-03 — `plane_min`/`plane_max` swing limits silently dropped at the translate boundary (no warn/comment) · NEW (informational)
- **Location**: `byroredux/src/ragdoll.rs:158-198` (`joint_from_imported`)
- **Mechanism**: `ImportedJointKind::Ragdoll` carries asymmetric `plane_min`/`plane_max` (decoded in `import/collision/ragdoll.rs::ragdoll_joint`) but `joint_from_imported` captures them into `..` and maps only cone/twist; `build_joint` applies symmetric `[-cone, cone]` on both swing axes. This is the documented physal.md §Known-approximation "cone→both swing axes" simplification — intentional — but unlike the sibling edge-drop sites (#1539/#1718/#1850, which `log::warn!`) this per-field loss is silent and uncommented.
- **Impact**: FNV ragdoll swing cones symmetric where authored data was asymmetric — fidelity gap, no runtime error.
- **Suggested fix**: one-line comment at `joint_from_imported` pointing at physal.md §Known-approximation + the symmetric-swing site. Full fix (map plane_min/max → Rapier AngY/AngZ) is planned rollout step 3.

#### DIM4-01 — ROADMAP structured-record total lags the live parse · NEW (doc)
- **Location**: `ROADMAP.md:76`
- **Detail**: ROADMAP headlines "73,054 structured records + 5,625 tail"; live `parse_rate_fnv_esm` reports `total=77,828` (roughly consistent, different bucketing — not a parse regression). Separately, the `14,881` in the compat matrix (`ROADMAP.md:206`) is the **NIF mesh** parse rate, not an ESM record count — the audit brief conflated the two. No code impact.
- **Suggested fix**: refresh the FNV structured-record total on next `/session-close`.

#### D8-01 — CLAUDE.md's FNV interior example uses bare `Meshes.bsa` / `Textures.bsa` (nonexistent in vanilla FNV) · NEW (doc)
- **Location**: `CLAUDE.md` Usage section (`--bsa Meshes.bsa --textures-bsa Textures.bsa`)
- **Detail**: vanilla FNV ships `Fallout - Meshes.bsa` / `Fallout - Textures.bsa`; no bare names exist. `--bsa <name>` opens the literal path relative to CWD with no prefix fallback, so running the CLAUDE.md example verbatim (even with correct CWD) opens **zero** archives → near-empty scene (~36 ent). `README.md:121-122` and `assets/debug_profiles.toml [profiles.fnv]` are both correct; drift is isolated to CLAUDE.md. Now surfaced loudly by the #1776 zero-archive guard rather than silently — papercut, not trap.
- **Suggested fix**: change the CLAUDE.md example to the quoted `"Fallout - …"` names (matching README), or to the `--game fnv` profile form.
